Important Guidelines

  • User-Friendly Interface: The job portal should have a clean and intuitive interface to make it easy for users to navigate and find relevant job listings.
  • Responsive Design: Ensure that the job portal is responsive and adapts to different screen sizes, including desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
  • Clear Search Functionality: Implement an effective search feature that allows users to search for jobs based on keywords, location, category, and other relevant filters.
  • Detailed Job Listings: Provide comprehensive job listings with all essential details such as job title, company name, location, job description, required qualifications, and application instructions.
  • User Registration and Login: Offer user registration and login options to enable users to save job searches, apply for jobs, and receive email notifications about relevant openings.
  • Personalized Job Recommendations: Use algorithms to provide personalized job recommendations based on the user's profile, skills, and preferences.
  • Easy Job Application Process: Simplify the job application process to encourage more candidates to apply. Include an option to upload resumes and cover letters.
